Circulo de Entablado

Circulo de Entablado aims to engage productivity in the collaborative process, build intellectual and aesthetic understanding of the craft and techniques of theatre arts, develop an appreciation of and respect for the various roles/aspects inherent within.

Student Council of Information Technology and Computing

SCITC represents IT students, advocating for their needs, fostering academic growth, and creating opportunities for leadership, innovation, and industry collaboration.

ISDA is an organization committed to empowering scholars through mentorship, skill development, and community engagement, fostering leadership and social responsibility.
