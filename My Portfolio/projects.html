PROJECTS

ManGROWV: Tech for a Greener Planet

ManGROWV was a mangrove tree planting activity organized and led by me as the President of SITE (Society of Information Technology Enthusiasts). I was responsible for the event coordination, leadership, and also the editing and layout of the promotional materials. The project aimed to promote environmental awareness and the role of technology in conservation efforts.

Ang Tatlumpong Taong mga Pangarap

A musical production by Circulo de Entablado (CDE), showcasing three decades of dreams, struggles, and triumphs. I was responsible for the layout designs used in the promotions, capturing the artistic essence of the musical. This project aimed to highlight the impact of theatre and storytelling in preserving culture and inspiring communities.
